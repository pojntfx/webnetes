@alphahorizonio/webnetes

webnetes (w6s)

A peer-to-peer computing platform for the browser and node. It uses WebAssembly for compute, WebRTC for networking and WebTorrent for storage.

Overview

Check out the documentation.

We also created a short demo video which shows an example application being deployed to webnetes:

Usage

webnetesctl

Recommended for most users.

webnetesctl Lite

For advanced users who prefer something more lightweight.
